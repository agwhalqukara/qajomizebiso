Early Life

Pierre was born on 21st June 1967 in Paris. His father was a surgeon, and his mother was a renowned academic who got her doctorate in linguistics. His parents moved to the U.S. while he was just a child. His mother, who taught Persian to students at Georgetown, separated from his father while he was a very young boy.

Omidyar graduated from Potomac, Maryland’s St. Andrew’s Episcopal School. He was interested in computers since childhood and invented a code to catalog the library books when he was only in the 9th standard. He was graduated from Tufts University in Somerville/Medford in Massachusetts with a Bachelors’s in computer science in the year 1988.

Career

After his graduation, Omidyar created a name for himself by upgrading MacDraw to MacDraw II. In the year 1991, he co-founded Ink Development, a pen-based computing company. Microsoft acquired the company in 1996.

He got the idea of buying and selling online, and on Labor Day in 1995, he launched Auction Web, which was later known as eBay, a site for global auctioning. He charged between 25 cents to $2 for posting on the site. He quit the job which he was doing then and gave his complete time to the newly founded company. Omidyar became the chairman of the company.

In the year 2010, Omidyar launched an investigative news service named “Honolulu Civil Beat.” For back to back three years, it was the Best News Website. He partnered with The Huffington Post in 2013 and launched the HuffPost Hawaii.

In 2014, he launched a journalism venture named First Look Media. It has some of the biggest reporters Glenn Greenwald, Dan Froomkin, Laura Poitras, and Jeremy Scahill, etc.

He is a well-known philanthropist too. He founded Omidyar Network in the year 2004 with his wife. It helps people to live life in a better way. They are also the members of The Giving Pledge of Warren Buffet. They joined hands with the Nduna Foundation to create “Enterprise Zimbabwe.” They donated a healthy sum of $115 to “Humanity United foundation.”

Awards & Achievements

Omidyar got some awards, too, for his tremendous achievements. Tufts University gave him the honorary doctorate in the year 2011. Before that, he is also the EY Entrepreneur of The Year National Winner in the year 1999.
